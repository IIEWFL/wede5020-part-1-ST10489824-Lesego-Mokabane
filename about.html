About United Youth League

Every Sport, Every Child, Every Dream

Who We Are

The United Youth League (UYL) is a community-based Multi-sport organization founded in 2012 by parents, coaches and sport enthusiasts. What started as a small tennis program serving 30 children has now grown into a thriving league with over 300 annual participants in tennis, basketball, cricket, soccer, and athletics.

We are entirely volunteer-run and depend on the enthusiasm of local coaches, parents, and community leaders. Our goal is to eliminate obstacles to sports, making sure that no child is left out due to their skill level or financial situation.

Our Mission

"Every Sport, Every Child, Every Dream."
Our mission is to encourage healthy lifestyles and character development through affordable and accessible youth sports programs.

Our Vision

To become a leading community hub where all children, regardless of skill or circumstances, can participate, grow,and develop into future leaders.
Where kids from different backgrounds can:

  • Find and develop their skills
  • Develop life skills and friendships.
  • Grow into future athletes, leaders, and exceptional role models.

Our Values

  • Teamwork - Develop into outstanding role models, future leaders, and athletes.
  • Respect - For opponents, teammates, coaches, and the actual game
  • Sportsmanship - We cherish fair play whether we win or lose.
  • Inclusivity - Develop into outstanding role models, future leaders, and athletes.
